About me

Hi, I'm Edlyn - an Arts graduate from the University of South Australia and a software developer in the making with _nology! I'm a self-taught artist and enjoy learning and all things creative.

After 5 years in the retail industry, I decided it was time for a change.

When I'm not writing code, you can find me watching anime, drawing, exploring the latest Netflix releases, playing a video game - oh! and trying to keep my new plant babies alive.
